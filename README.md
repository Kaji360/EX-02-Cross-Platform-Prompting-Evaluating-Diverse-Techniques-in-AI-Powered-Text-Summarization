# EX-02: Cross-Platform Prompting – Evaluating Diverse Techniques in AI-Powered Text Summarization
## AIM :

To evaluate and compare the effectiveness of different prompting techniques such as Zero-Shot, Few-Shot, Chain-of-Thought, and Role-Based Prompting across multiple AI platforms including OpenAI ChatGPT, Google Gemini, Anthropic Claude, and Microsoft Copilot for AI-powered text summarization tasks.

## SCENARIO :

You are part of a content curation team for an educational platform that provides short summaries of research papers for undergraduate students. The task is to summarize a 500-word technical article titled “The Basics of Blockchain Technology” using different AI platforms and prompting strategies.

## The experiment aims to identify the best combination of prompting technique and AI platform based on:

Accuracy
Coherence
Simplicity
Speed
User Experience
ALGORITHM
### Step 1:

Select a 500-word technical article on Blockchain Technology.

### Step 2:

### Choose different AI platforms:

OpenAI ChatGPT
Google Gemini
Anthropic Claude
Microsoft Copilot
Step 3:

### Prepare different prompting techniques:

Zero-Shot Prompting
Few-Shot Prompting
Chain-of-Thought Prompting
Role-Based Prompting
### Step 4:

Enter the same article into each AI platform using each prompting technique.

### Step 5:

Generate summaries from all platforms.

### Step 6:

### Compare the generated summaries based on:

Accuracy
Clarity
Coherence
Simplicity
Response Speed
User Experience
### Step 7:

Record observations and identify the best-performing platform and prompting method.

## RESULT :

The experiment demonstrated that prompting techniques greatly influence the quality of AI-generated summaries. Among the tested methods, Role-Based Prompting and Chain-of-Thought Prompting produced more accurate, coherent, and student-friendly summaries.

OpenAI ChatGPT and Anthropic Claude provided the best overall performance in terms of readability, simplicity, and user experience. Zero-Shot Prompting generated faster outputs, while Few-Shot Prompting improved formatting consistency.

Hence, the experiment concluded that selecting the appropriate prompting technique and AI platform significantly improves the effectiveness of AI-powered text summarization.
